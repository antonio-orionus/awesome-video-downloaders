# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions.
- Focus feedback on ideas and content, not on people.
- Accept that maintainers have final say on inclusion decisions.
- No spam, self-promotion outside of legitimate contributions, or harassment of any kind.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces: issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in comments being removed or contributors being blocked at the maintainers' discretion.
